Privacy Policy

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, browse the marketplace, or make a purchase. This includes your name, email address, and payment information processed through our secure partners.

Additionally, we automatically collect certain information when you visit BuyNext, such as your IP address, device type, and browsing patterns to help improve your experience.

verified_user Our Data Promise

We never sell your personal data to third-party advertisers. We believe your privacy is not a product. Your trust is our most valuable asset, and we protect it with industry-standard encryption.

Your data is used strictly to personalize your shopping experience, fulfill orders, and prevent fraudulent activity on the platform. Specifically, we use it for:

We use cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. You can manage your cookie settings through your browser at any time, though some features of BuyNext may not function correctly without them.

Depending on your location, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact our privacy team at privacy@buynext.com.
