Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the BuyNext experience. Can't find what you're looking for? Chat with us.

How do I track my order?

add
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email containing a tracking number and a link to our global logistics portal. You can also view your order status in real-time within your account dashboard under "Order History."

Are the products authentic?

add
Absolutely. Every seller on BuyNext undergoes a rigorous manual verification process. We verify business licenses, product origins, and quality standards. We offer a 100% Authenticity Guarantee; if an item is proven to be inauthentic, we provide a full refund plus shipping costs.

What is your return policy?

add
We offer a 30-day "no-questions-asked" return policy for items in their original condition and packaging. Simply visit our Returns Center, print your pre-paid shipping label, and drop it off at any authorized courier location.

Do you ship internationally?

add
Yes, BuyNext currently delivers to over 50 countries. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location, but most orders arrive within 7-12 business days. Import duties are calculated at checkout so there are no hidden surprises upon delivery.

How can I contact a seller directly?

add
On every product page, there is a "Message Seller" button located just below the product description. This allows you to ask specific questions about sizing, materials, or custom orders directly to the artisan.

Our dedicated support team is ready to assist you 24/7.
