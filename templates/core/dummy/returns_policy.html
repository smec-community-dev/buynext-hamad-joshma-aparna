Policy Center

Returns & Refunds.

We want you to love what you bought. If you don't, we've made the return process as simple as the purchase.

30-Day Window

Items must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund.

Original Condition

Products must be unused, unwashed, and in their original premium packaging.

01. Initiate

Online Portal

Log in to your BuyNext Dashboard. Navigate to 'Order History', select the item you wish to return, and click 'Request Return'. You will receive a pre-paid shipping label immediately via email.

02. Pack

Sustainability First

Place the item back in its original box. We encourage you to reuse the shipping mailer we provided to reduce environmental impact. Affix the printed label to the outside of the package.

info Ensure no old shipping labels are visible on the box.

03. Refund

The Payback

Once our artisan partners receive and inspect the item (usually within 48 hours of arrival), we will issue a refund to your original payment method. Depending on your bank, this may take 5-10 business days to appear.

Non-Returnable Items

  • / Custom-made or personalized jewelry
  • / Digital gift cards and downloadable software
  • / Intimate apparel or hygiene-sensitive items
  • / Final Sale items marked at 50% discount or higher
